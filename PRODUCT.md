# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, CTOs, founders, and senior engineers evaluating Grishmin for contract or full-time frontend work. They view this in a browser on desktop or mobile, often in a quiet office or at a desk, actively comparing candidates. Context: decision-making mode, short attention span, pattern-matching for signal.

## Product Purpose

A portfolio that communicates Grishmin Karki's craft, judgment, and seniority as a frontend engineer. Success is a recruiter or founder reaching out. The portfolio itself demonstrates the skills it claims: pixel-perfect execution, design literacy, and attention to detail.

## Brand Personality

Confident, rigorous, unhurried. Not flashy-portfolio, not humble-humble. The work speaks; the design amplifies it.

## Anti-references

- Generic dev portfolio (big emoji headers, card grids, hero with confetti)
- Over-animated agency sites
- Dark mode for the sake of dark mode (no personality behind it)
- Cream/sand/linen AI-generated editorial warmth

## Design Principles

1. Practice what you preach — the portfolio is itself evidence of frontend craft
2. Show, don't decorate — every visual choice serves communication, not aesthetics-for-aesthetics
3. Confident negative space — give ideas room to land; resist filling every pixel
4. One accent, used deliberately — lime (#C6F24E) earns its moments

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced-motion respected on all animations. Focus states visible. Color not used as the sole information carrier.
